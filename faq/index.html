Hackathon FAQ

What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is an event where individuals or teams collaborate intensively on software projects within a limited timeframe.
Who can participate?
Anyone with an interest in programming and problem-solving can participate. Typically, participants include developers, designers, and business enthusiasts.
How can I register for the hackathon?
Registration details will be provided on our website. Keep an eye on our announcements and social media channels for updates.
Do I need a team to participate?
While you can participate individually, forming a team is encouraged. It allows for diverse skills and perspectives, enhancing the overall experience.
